# Process Automation Toolkit Evidence Checklist

Use this checklist before putting the case on a CV, portfolio, or into interviews.

## Already Supported By The Repository

- Internal browser-side workflow automation positioning
- Editorial/CMS operator workflow focus
- Reusable command-driven toolkit framing
- Shared UI/launch surface framing
- Content/admin/media workflow coverage
- Validation and human-in-the-loop guardrails
- Structured export/handoff flow
- AI-assisted development and handoff evidence
- Maintainability and architecture-doc signals

## Should Be Collected Next

### High priority

- Count of user-facing commands
- Count of active workflow modules
- Count of fields validated/normalized in admin flows
- One sample artifact set from source-export/handoff flow
- One sample report/crawler output artifact
- 1-2 screenshots of the operator UI/launch surface
- 1-2 short before/after workflow walkthroughs

### Medium priority

- A measured example of step reduction for one workflow
- A measured example of time reduction for one workflow
- Commit-history slices that show iterative growth over time
- Any internal notes showing that the toolkit was reused across multiple workflow areas

### Nice to have

- Screenshots of validation/guardrail states
- Example of a manual-review checkpoint being surfaced
- Notes on which flows were used most often

## Evidence By Audience

### Recruiter

Needs:
- clear one-line description
- understandable internal-tool value
- confidence that the case is real and scoped appropriately

### Hiring manager

Needs:
- workflow problem
- solution shape
- ownership level
- signs of judgment and maintainability

### Technical interviewer

Needs:
- architecture boundaries
- tradeoffs
- constraints
- why the approach was reasonable

## Claims To Use Only With Extra Evidence

- exact adoption size
- exact time savings
- exact business impact
- enterprise/platform-scale language
- broad team/process ownership claims

## Quick Readiness Check

You are ready to use the case confidently if you have:
- the long report;
- the career pack;
- a role-tailored variant;
- interview Q&A;
- at least a couple of screenshots or artifacts;
- a conservative answer for impact that avoids invented numbers.
